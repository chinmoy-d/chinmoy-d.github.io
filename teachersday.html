Teachers' Day 2020

Delhi Public School, Digboi

About Program

Virtual Teacher's Day 2020 Celebration of Delhi Public School, Digboi. An event organised with love by students of Class XII.

Let's have a glance!

This being our last year at DPS Digboi, we have missed out on many a thing due to the ongoing unrest. But, we couldn't have missed something like the last Teacher's Day at school, that we always have dreamt of since kindergarten. Although, we'll be missing wearing Sarees, Heels and Tuxedoes, we're still trying our hands on celebrating it virtually adhering to COVID-19 protocols.
